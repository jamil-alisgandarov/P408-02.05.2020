Azerbaijan confirms 50 more coronavirus cases; 1 dies and 40 recover

Fifty more cases of the novel coronavirus (COVID-19) have been recorded in Azerbaijan,
while 40 people have recovered from the infection, the Operational Headquarters under the Cabinet of Ministers reported Friday.
